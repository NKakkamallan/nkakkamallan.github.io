Academic Streams

AkshayaH offers two streams of teaching-learning structures which cater to different kinds of personalised learning needs.

Formal Stream Flexible Stream

Formal Stream

Timing

Full-time school with maximum class strength of 18-20 per section

Curriculum

The curriculum is rolled out in definite time cycles and structures, with periodic formative and summative assessments.

Assessment

Differentiated teaching and assessment are practised to address the unique needs and potentials of all students in a regular classroom setting.

Flexible Stream

Timing

designed to cater to the needs of those who need to complete their education through a part-time or flexible schedule.

Pros

This has worked well for students who want to pursue their passion in performing arts, professional sports and so on while they continue their schooling on par with their peers. 

Assessment

Learning and assessment happen through a combination of online and on-site sessions with every effort made to allow for participation in class activities across curricular and co-curricular classes.

Other benefits

Students with medical restrictions could also benefit.
